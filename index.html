The process
How It Works
01

Build your game

Create an original game. Browser, itch.io, .exe, or any other upload site. It needs to be playable and open source on GitHub.

02

Design your character

Model an original 3D character that appears in the game. Export a .stl file of them too for later!

03

Log your hours

Use Hackatime to track your time with its plugins to track time in your 3D apps too!.

04

Ship it

Submit your repo, game link, character, and .stl file. We verify and ship prizes to your door.
